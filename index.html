The Story of May Coffee

Welcome to May Tea & Coffee. Our mission is simple: to bring you back to nature through the purest ingredients. We meticulously source our tea leaves and coffee beans directly from responsible farms to ensure every cup delivers a cloud-like smoothness.

We are a dedicated international chain of Matcha desserts, rooting from traditional Japanese tea culture. By upholding standard craftsmanship and modern dedication, establish Matcha culture as our core brand. Experience the purity of clouds in every sip.
